Privacy Policy - Army Calendar App

Data Collection

This app does not collect personal data or transmit it to third parties.

Backup/Restore

  • Users can create encrypted backups of their app databases.
  • The backup files are stored locally on the user's device.
  • Restore is performed locally and requires the user-provided password.

Security

  • All backups are encrypted using AES.
  • Passwords are not stored by the app.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer.
